Culture & Heritage

The Soul of the Capital

Beijing's culture is a rich tapestry woven over thousands of years, carrying the profound heritage of the Chinese civilization.

  • Peking Opera

    A stylized Chinese form of traditional theatre combining music, vocal performance, mime, and acrobatics.

  • Hutongs & Courtyards

    Narrow alleys that form the historic residential layout, preserving the authentic traditional lifestyle of locals.

  • Intangible Cultural Heritage

    Home to master crafts like Cloisonné (Jingtailan), carved lacquerware, and traditional paper cutting.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Autumn (September to November) offers pleasant weather with brilliant colors. Spring is also beautiful, though sometimes windy.

Transportation

The Beijing Subway is vast, fast, and economical. Taxis are plentiful, and bicycle sharing is everywhere.

Payment Mode

Mobile payments (WeChat Pay and Alipay) are ubiquitous. It is recommended to link a foreign credit card prior to arrival.
